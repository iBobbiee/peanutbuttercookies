Gluten Free Peanut Butter Cookies

Rebecca's favorite cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp coarse salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Line large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. Make 1 tbsp balls of dough and place on baking sheet.
  5. Flatten balls with a fork, making a criss-cross pattern.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
  7. Cool on baking racks.
