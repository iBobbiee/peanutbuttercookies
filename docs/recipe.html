name of the website Gluten Free Peanut Butter Cookies Rebecca's favorite cookie recipe. Ingredients 1 cup natural peanut butter 1 cup of sugar 1 tsp vanilla 1 egg 1 tbsp coarse salt Directions Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Line large baking sheet with parchment paper. Make 1 tbsp balls of dough and place on baking sheet. Flatten balls with a fork, making a criss-cross pattern. Bake for 10 minutes. Cool on baking racks.
